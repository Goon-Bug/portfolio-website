Gin Rummy Showcase

A game that imitates the classic card game Gin Rummy.
Made in Pygame.

  • Next

Local Multiplayer

A server can be started using Python sockets and threads to allow players to connect with one another locally.

  • Next

Starting Play

Once both player's are connected, the game will deal out 10 cards to each player. The aim of the game is to draw and discard cards until a player has enough sets that their deadwood reaches below 10.

The game will automatically highlight cards that are in sets. Player's can rearrange their hand by pressing the sort button or by manually selecting cards to swap.

  • Next

How to Win

Once a player has reached below 10 for their respective deadwood score the player will have the option to knock. Once pressed, the end score will be calculated. The player with the least deadwood score wins.

If a player can go out with 0 deadwood score then they will have the option to call gin. If gin occurs then they will automatically win the round and get a bonus 25 points.

  • Next

End of Round

Once a player has pressed a win button then the end of round scoreboard will be shown. This display's each of the player's final hands and calculates their score.

If the winning player reaches a score of 50 or above then they win the game! If not another round is played until a player finally reaches a score of 50
