Work Hours Calculator Showcase

An application that allows users to store your work shifts and displays their total hours and pay for the week.

  • Next

Input Work Times

Users can input their work hours, which will be stored in an SQL database. The date and pay rate can be easily updated through the corresponding buttons.

The holiday button allows users to automatically insert their work shift for the specified day.

  • Next

Save your Shift Times

Users can save/change their shift times with the 'Change Shifts' button. The shift times will be updated in the database.

  • Next

Breaks

Users will be able to enter their companies threshold for breaks.

"For example, in this demo, to qualify for a 45-minute break, a person must work 8 hours and 10 minutes or more.

  • Next

Delete Shifts

Users can delete shifts from the database by entering the date of their shift.
