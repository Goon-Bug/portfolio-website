Date Spark Showcase

Date Spark is a Flutter-based mobile application with a Python backend, designed to help users discover and save date ideas through a token-based spinning wheel system.

Featuring local SQLite storage, JWT authentication, and a retro 80s-inspired UI, the app integrates ad-based monetization and client-side caching for an smooth user experience.

Register

Users can register for the app using their email address and a password. Once registered and verified, they can log in to the app using the same credentials.

The app uses JWT authentication to ensure secure access to user data.

  • Next

Login

Users can log in to the app using their registered email address and password. Once logged in, they can access the main features of the app.

  • Next

Spinning The Wheel!

Once logged in, users will have access to the main feature of the app, the spinning wheel.

Spinning the wheel will generate a random date idea. Date Ideas are generic for now but will be updated with real world Date Ideas by use of buyable city packs. Each city pack will have real world date ideas for that city.

Tags are used to categorize the date ideas, allowing users to filter their search results.

  • Next

Change Profile Picture

Users can change their profile picure from a selction of icons.

Once the user has selected their profile picture, it is saved to the database and displayed on their profile.

The app uses SQLite for local storage, ensuring that user data is stored securely and efficiently.

  • Next

Tokens

Users can earn tokens by watching ads.

Tokens are used to spin the wheel and generate date ideas.

User will get a free spin every 24 hours.

  • Next

Timeline

Users can easily keep track their favorite dates by adding them to their timeline.

Users will do this by selecting a date idea from the app's curated list, writing a description, uploading a photo, then choosing the date they experienced it.

They will then be able to see the list of dates they have been on in a timeline format. The images can be clicked on to view in full.
